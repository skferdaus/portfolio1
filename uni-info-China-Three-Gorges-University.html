China Three Gorges University

Overview

Established in 2000, China Three Gorges University is a public higher education institution located in the large city of Yichang (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants), Hubei. Officially accredited and/or recognized by the Department of Education, Hubei Province, China Three Gorges University (CTGU) is a large (uniRank enrollment range: 20,000-24,999 students) coeducational higher education institution. China Three Gorges University (CTGU) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees such as bachelor degrees in several areas of study. See the uniRank degree levels and areas of study matrix below for further details. International applicants are eligible to apply for enrollment.
